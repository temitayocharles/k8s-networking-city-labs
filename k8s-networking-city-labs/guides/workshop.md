# Workshop Guide: Kubernetes Networking City Analogy

## Structure
- Begin with a city model overview and glossary.
- Progress through Linux labs, then Kubernetes equivalents.
- Use visuals and analogies throughout.

## Tips
- Encourage hands-on experimentation.
- Use troubleshooting guide for common issues.
- Relate every technical step to the city analogy.

## Example Flow
1. Introduce apartments, buildings, and hallways.
2. Build up to overlay networks and services.
3. Explore security and failure scenarios.
4. End with a Q&A and open troubleshooting session.
